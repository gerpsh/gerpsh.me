eBridge to Health

In a project spanning ~1 year, I along with a group of designers (Bryan Blake, Lawrence Chang, Andrew Miller) designed a mobile application and prototype device to help cigarette smokers kick the habit. Drawing on my experience in smoking cessation clinical research, we designed the mobile app to help smokers mitigate negative emotion and gain insight into how their environment affects their mental state and their desire to smoke. To make data collection more efficient, we also designed cigarette smoke detector using an Arduino to detect cigarette smoke and differentiate it from other forms of carbon-monoxide containing gases. I also designed a back-end for the mobile application in preparation for full implementation.
