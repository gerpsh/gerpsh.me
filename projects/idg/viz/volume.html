Volume


Moving on the volume. Let's say I magically make the box bigger. This means that the particles will be colliding with the sides of the box less often. Use the slider to see what happens when you changes the volume:



You'll notice that the pressure doesn't change linearly; this is because when you rearrage the equation to solve for P, V is underneath everything, and P will decrease more and more slowly as V increases.
