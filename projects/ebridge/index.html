eBridge to Health

During the school year 2014-2015, I was able to work on the eBridge to Health project at the University of Michigan Medical School. The eBridge to Health is an ongoing clinical research study testing the effectiveness of an online platform meant to help college students find mental health resources. I worked with Kai Zheng to build and maintain the platform using C#/ASP.NET and SQL Server.
